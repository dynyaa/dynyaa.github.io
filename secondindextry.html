Diversity of the arsenal


Dribbling


Previous Next

Dribbling is maneuvering a ball by one player while moving in a given direction, avoiding defenders' attempts to intercept the ball. Dribbling is an attacking move, where the player should retain the ball with them, until the opponent is defeated.

Special skills


Previous Next

Feint is a player deceiving the opposition into believing he is going to pass, shoot, move in a certain direction, or receive the ball and instead doing something entirely different, thus gaining an advantage



How to get better in dribbling?





Master the basics


  •           Make gentle contact with the ball

  •           Keep the ball close to your feet

  •           Do long-distence drills

Hone your skills


  •           Train often

  •           Experience new techniques

  •           Be patient. Even Rome wasn't built             in a day

Practice in real game


  •           Practice makes perfect

  •           Learn from your mistakes

  •           Be confident and try new things
